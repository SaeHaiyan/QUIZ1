Local Tourist Places in
Johor Darul Takzim

The state of Johor, located in the south of Peninsular Malaysia, is the main gateway to Malaysia via Singapore, thus stealing the attention of tourists from within and outside the country. This great state not only thriving in terms of development and modernization but is also graced with beautiful island and beaches as well as biodiversity landscapes that have been preserved for thousands of years.

Heres several tourist attraction places that exist in Johor:


Legoland Malaysia

Legoland Malaysia Resort amusement park can be one of the family tourist attractions in Malaysia. The location is at 7 Jalan Legoland, 79100 Iskandar Puteri, Johor, Malaysia. It is about 23 kilometers from the Johor area, with a travel time of about 30 minutes by motor vehicle.

Puteri Harbour

Puteri Harbour is home to the southernmost marina on the mainland of continent Asia, making it a must visit destination on the world sailing map. Located at the narrow-most point between Singapore and Johor; known as the Puteri Narrows, Malaysia - lies the breathtaking Marinas at Puteri Harbour. The Public Marina, Private Marina & Mega Yacht Marina can accomodate yachts with LOA up to 90 meters”.

Tanjung Piai National Park

Also known as Johor National Park, this Johor attraction is a national heritage site of Johor and a RAMSAR site (a designated wetland site of international importance). The 926 hectares of wetlands and mudflats where more than 29 species of mangroves lie. Our guide led us through the 1.7 kilometre-long boardwalk, and pointed out the array of fauna that call this park its home.

Firefly Park

Adventure Waterpark Desaru Coast is one of the largest water parks in Malaysia, located in Desaru, Johor. It offers a variety of thrilling water rides and attractions for all ages, making it a perfect destination for families and adventure seekers. Adventure Waterpark Desaru Coast promises a fun-filled day under the sun, catering to both thrill-seekers and those looking to relax.
